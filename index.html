About Me

My Story

I’m Karan Arjun S, from Chennai. I began as a beginner with curiosity and limited resources. Over time, through trial-and-error, I learned to read datasheets, solder SMD parts, write firmware, and design simple mechanical fixtures. This site records that journey.

What I Believe

If we try, learn, and share, the next breakthrough can come from anywhere — even a single person in Madras. My goal is to inspire, teach, and collaborate.

How to Use this Site

Browse projects, check the learning log for tutorials, and contact me for collaborations. Everything here is built and written by me — raw, honest, and practical.

Vision

More than a Portfolio

Madras Silicons is a statement — showcasing the power of curiosity, persistence, and making. I want to build a community where makers from India can learn, collaborate, and push boundaries.
